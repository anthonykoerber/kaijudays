Chicago Toy Show

2023 marks the 50th year of Chicago Toy Show, held 3 times per year at the Kane County Fairgrounds in St Charles, IL. Chicago Toy Show is the largest specialty show consisting only of antique, vintage, & doll collectibles. You'll find 7 buildings and 1 pavilion full of exhibitors & shoppers from around the world.
