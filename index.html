Chicago Toy Show - Spring 2022

4/24 St Charles, IL - The Kaiju Days Mega Booth invades another Chicago Toy Show! We'll be bringing the best Kaiju, Tokusatsu & Super Robot collectibles in every size, shape, and price range.

The fourth Sunday of each April, July, and October Chicago Toy Show is held at the Kane County Fairgrounds. 2022 marks the 49th year of the largest specialty show of vintage & antique toy and doll collectibles. There will be 7 buildings full of exhibitors from around the world.
