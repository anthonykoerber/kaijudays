The Great Ohio Toy Show - Spring 2022

3/26 Xenia, OH - The Kaiju Days pop-up experience stomps into another Great Ohio Toy Show! We'll be bringing the best Kaiju, Tokusatsu & Super Robot collectibles in every size, shape, and price range.

The Great Ohio Toy show features over 500 tables of toys of all kinds, with dealers from across the country! One of the largest toy shows in the Midwest - You'll find vintage toys, modern toys, comics, cards, diecast, dolls, & pop culture collectibles of kinds. Food trucks will be on site for an authentic carnival midway experience!

About Us

KAIJU DAYS is a boutique pop-up event celebrating Japanese Monsters and Robots. Our dealers offer collectible toys, apparel, posters and original artwork. Come join the tribe!

KAIJU DAYS is hosted by @halfbakedfantasy & @otakumerica and will appear in random locations throughout Chicagoland and the Midwest.
